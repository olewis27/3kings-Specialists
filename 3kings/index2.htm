Welcome to 3Kings Upper Extremity Specialists

 

3Kings Upper Extremity Specialists would like to thank everyone who attended the shoulder and wrist course. We are pleased to announce the next course that will be held in Jacksonville. It will be on static splinting and we have partnered with Orfit industries to bring this course to the area. Sign up early, there is only space for 24 therapists and you can receive an early bird discount if you register by October 1st, 2008. Also, if you have attended one of our previous courses, you will receive a 10% discount on your registration. All the information you need on the course and registration is found on this website, but if you have further questions, do not hesitate to contact us.

We look forward to seeing you on November 1, 2008 at the Best Western Hotel on Southpoint, centrally located just off of I-95 and J Turner Butler Boulevard.
