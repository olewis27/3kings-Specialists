Welcome to 3Kings Upper Extremity Specialists


There is only 1 course left for the year. I hope that you will join 3Kings Upper Extremity Specialists as we host for the 2nd year Doctor’s Demystify-The Thumb. I hope you all will take advantage of learning from 6 area hand and plastic surgeons who have taken time out of their busy schedules to educate area therapists on the thumb. This year the course is limited to 30 participants due to the size of the clinic so please early.  A big thank you to In Motion Physical Therapy for being the host facility this year! This smaller venue will allow you to be up close and personal with the surgeons. The panel sessions will allow you to ask questions and get each of the surgeon’s perspectives on each topic. Cost is only $99. Faulty includes:

*Marc Kaye, MD-University of Florida Health
*Asher Smith, MD-NAS Jax
*Brett Puckett, MD-Southeast Orthopedics
*Chris Goll, MD-Southeast Orthopedics
*Antonio Forte, MD, MHS, PhD-Mayo Clinic Jacksonville
*Richard Curtis, MD-Hand and Wrist Center

Please remember that 3 Kings is not taking registrations for the Doctor’s Demystify. You must register on www.doctorsdemystify.com.
